Ryan Coronado started making music at 16, DJing in LA's underground rave and house party scene. What started as 6-hour sets on weekends developed into a finer appreciation for club culture, audio engineering, and electronic music production. His sound has grown to become a funky blend of Deep House and Minimal Techno that makes for a perfectly energized dance floor. As an avid concert-goer, he takes inspiration from both US and European House music cultures, conveying his emotional experiences with music and vision for life on the dance floor in both his sets and original productions. Now 21, Ryan Coronado has performed in Boston, MA; Stockholm, Sweden; and Los Angeles, CA, where he continues to advance his musicianship at the UCLA Herb Alpert School of Music.
